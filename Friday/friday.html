Procedure for baking cake

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Margerine
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Egg

Procedure

  1. put your butter in a bowl
  2. add two cups of sugar
  3. mix until sugar blends with butter
  4. add your milk, eggs and other wet ingredients
  5. add your flour
  6. mix till you get a consistent texture
